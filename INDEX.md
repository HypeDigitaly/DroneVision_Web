## The 5 Core Documents

### 1. PRD_TEMPLATE.md (8,500+ words)

**The actual PRD template you'll use for every project.**

**Structure:**
- Section 1: Product Overview
- Section 2: Client Input Checklist (enhanced with pricing, target audience, lead magnet, IČO/DIČ, Cal.com, Google Reviews)
- Section 3: Technical Architecture (includes VSL specs - YouTube facade, mobile layout, GA4 tracking)
- Section 4: Critical Features
  - 4.1-4.9: Standard features (i18n, forms, admin, SEO, CRO)
  - **4.10: CRO Copy Patterns** (PAS flow, benefit-driven headlines, CTA patterns)
  - **4.11: Czech Language (Čeština) Standards** (vykání rules, native tone, terminology)
  - **4.12: Hero Section - Alex Hormozi $100M Offers Framework** (value equation, VSL layout)
- Section 5: Analytics & Tracking
- Section 6: Content Template
- Section 7: Implementation Checklist
- Section 8: Success Metrics
- Section 9: Environment Variables
- Section 10: Deployment & Hosting
- Section 11: Maintenance & Support
- Section 12: Team Roles & Responsibilities

**How to use:**
1. Make a copy: `PRD_[ClientName]_v2.0.md`
2. Fill in all `[CLIENT_TO_FILL]` sections
3. Share Sections 2 & 6 with client (reference new prerequisites in 2.4, VSL specs in 2.5/3.6)
4. Apply Sections 4.10-4.12 to copy structure (CRO patterns, Czech vykání, Hormozi framework)
5. Use Section 7 as working checklist during development
6. Monitor Section 8 metrics post-launch

**Best for:** Developers, product managers doing actual work

## How to Use This System

### For Your First Project

**Day 1:**
1. Read PRD_TEMPLATE_SUMMARY.md (3 min)
2. Read PRD_QUICK_START.md (5 min)
3. Understand what you're doing

**Day 2:**
1. Read PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Customization Guide" section (10 min)
2. Copy PRD_TEMPLATE.md to `PRD_[ClientName]_v2.0.md`
3. Skim through template to understand structure

**Days 3-5:**
1. Have kickoff meeting with client
2. Use Sections 1, 2, 8, 12 to guide conversation
3. Get client input on all `[CLIENT_TO_FILL]` items

**Week 2:**
1. Tech team reviews Section 3 (30 min)
2. Customize Sections 4, 6, 8 (3 hours)
3. Share with client for approval
4. Begin project per Section 7 phases

### For Your Second Project

**Day 1:**
1. Copy PRD_TEMPLATE.md to `PRD_[NewClient]_v2.0.md`
2. Customize Sections 1, 2, 6, 8 (2 hours)
3. Share with client

**Days 2-5:**
1. Kickoff meeting
2. Technical setup
3. Start development

**Time saved:** 28 hours (vs. first project)

### For Your Fifth Project

**Same process, but:**
- Customization takes 1.5 hours (familiar with template)
- You reuse components from previous projects
- Team knows the process
- Predictable timeline and deliverables

**Cumulative time saved:** 140+ hours across 5 projects

## Navigation by Situation

### "I'm starting a new project. What do I do?"

1. Read PRD_QUICK_START.md "30-Second Summary"
2. Follow "5 Easy Steps"
3. Copy PRD_TEMPLATE.md
4. Customize for your client (check new prerequisites in Section 2.4, VSL specs in 2.5/3.6)
5. Apply CRO copy patterns (4.10), language standards (4.11 for Czech), and Hormozi framework (4.12)
6. Go

### "I don't understand the template structure."

1. Read README_PRD_TEMPLATE_SYSTEM.md "Template Architecture Explained"
2. Read PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Section-by-Section Guidance"
3. Skim PRD_TEMPLATE.md to see examples

### "How do I customize for [industry]?"

1. Check PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Common Customization Scenarios"
2. Or search README_PRD_TEMPLATE_SYSTEM.md for your industry
3. Adapt sections as shown in examples

### "What if we only have 4 weeks?"

1. Read PRD_QUICK_START.md "Red Flags" section
2. Check README_PRD_TEMPLATE_SYSTEM.md "Project Timeline Reference"
3. Refer to PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Common Customization Scenarios" > "Minimal Scope"
4. Reduce scope accordingly

### "Can we use [different tech]?"

1. Read README_PRD_TEMPLATE_SYSTEM.md "Why It's Fixed"
2. Read PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Troubleshooting: Client Insists on Different Tech Stack"
3. Make decision with team

### "We're stuck. What do we do?"

1. Check Section 7 of PRD_TEMPLATE.md (what should be done now?)
2. Check Section 11 of PRD_TEMPLATE.md "Troubleshooting"
3. Check PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Troubleshooting"
4. Ask the team

### "How do we track progress?"

1. Use Section 7 of PRD_TEMPLATE.md as checklist
2. Track in Asana/GitHub Projects/Trello
3. Reference Section 8 for success metrics
4. Weekly standup using Section 7 progress

### "Project's done. What now?"

1. Follow Section 7, Phase 12 checklist (testing & launch)
2. Verify CRO Copy Patterns (4.10) and language standards (4.11) were applied
3. Monitor Section 8 metrics post-launch (especially Hormozi hero framework performance)
4. Use Section 11 for ongoing maintenance
5. Archive this PRD for reference on next project (reuse CRO patterns, language standards, framework)

## FAQ

**Q: Which document should I read first?**

A: PRD_TEMPLATE_SUMMARY.md (3 min), then PRD_QUICK_START.md (5 min)

**Q: Do I have to read all 5 documents?**

A: No. Read based on your role (see "Reading Guide by Role" section above)

**Q: Can I just use the template without reading other docs?**

A: Yes, but you'll be slower. First-time reading takes 1-2 hours. Saves 20+ hours on project.

**Q: Where do I get help?**

A: Check PRD_TEMPLATE_GUIDE.md "Troubleshooting" first

**Q: How is this different from other templates?**

A: It's based on a real, production website. It includes implementation checklist. It's meant to be used by developers, not just PMs.

**Q: Can I modify this template?**

A: Yes. After using it once, you'll have ideas for improvements. Update and share back to team.
